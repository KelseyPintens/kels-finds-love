01 — about

A little about me

I've built a life I really love, and I'm looking for someone who has one of their own. I'm happiest somewhere between a deep conversation and a spontaneous adventure — a road trip, a farmers market morning, cooking together, or stargazing somewhere quiet.

I love making things with my hands (currently ceramics… maybe someday a little lifestyle brand), spending time outside, exploring national parks, and hosting friends.
